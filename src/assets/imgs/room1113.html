About this area

Kempton Park

Located in Kempton Park, Protea Hotel by Marriott O.R. Tambo Airport is in the business district and connected to the airport. Victory Theater and Satyagraha House are local landmarks, and some of the area's activities can be experienced at Kempton Park Golf Club and Modderfontein Golf Club. Looking to enjoy an event or a game while in town? See what's going on at Johannesburg Stadium, or consider a night out at POPArt Theatre. Our guests appreciate the hotel's quiet location.

About this property

Protea Hotel by Marriott O.R. Tambo Airport

This hotel features a restaurant, an outdoor pool, and a 24-hour fitness center. Free WiFi in public areas is provided, as well as free self parking, a free airport shuttle, and free train station pick-up. Other amenities include a bar/lounge, a conference center, and coffee/tea in a common area. All 213 soundproofed rooms feature free WiFi and Tempur-Pedic beds. For entertainment, LCD TVs come with satellite channels, and guests can also appreciate comforts like premium bedding and down comforters.
